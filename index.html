About Me.

I am a 20 year old, ex-Digital Media Student, and current Epicodus student following the PHP/Drupal tract for back-end development.

Education and experience

My education history includes two years at College of the Redwoods in Eureka, California, and years of personal experience as a tech enthusiest.

College experience

I attended College of the Redwoods for two years. I was studying for an associates degree in Digital Media, specializing in Animation. During my time at College of the Redwoods I learned various things which are relevant to my future career in front and back-end development.

  • Web Design Principles
  • 2D, hand-drawn animation
  • 3D modeling and animation
  • Web development with HTML and CSS
  • Photo and vector editing skills
  • Video editing skills

While at college I learned various things and neared the completion of my degree, however I never completed it due to a scheduling error which would result in a year gap before I could take my final required classes. This, combined with a desire to move on from the college experience, caused me to be open to other options, like code bootcamps.

I heard about Epicodus from a friend who knew that I was interested in technology and coding, who told me about a friend who had great success going through Epicodus and subsequently finding a job after.
